## LinkedIn-specific guidance

**Posting time:** Tuesday or Wednesday, 8:00–10:00 AM in your audience's primary timezone. For mixed US+EU, target 8:00 AM EST = mid-afternoon CET = lunch in Asia for Singapore/HK freelance community.

**Format tricks:**
- Bullet points use real bullets (•) and arrows (→) — LinkedIn renders them well
- Short paragraphs (1–2 lines) — long blocks get auto-collapsed under "...see more"
- The first 3 lines are critical (above the fold) — the photographer hook front-loaded
- Hashtags at the end, max 3 — more reads as spam
- No links in the first 4 paragraphs (LinkedIn algorithm penalizes early external links — placing it after the bullets is safer)

**Engagement tactics:**
- Reply to every comment in the first 2 hours — boosts reach algorithmically
- Ask a question in your first 5 replies ("what tool combo do you use today?") to invite chain replies
- Don't edit the post in the first hour — edits reset reach scoring on LinkedIn

**Follow-up post (Day +3, optional):**
```
24-hour update: [N] freelancers signed up to try LancerWise.

What surprised me — most of them didn't ask about the AI. They asked about the multi-currency invoicing.

Lesson: I built the feature that excited me. They paid for the feature that solved their actual problem.

The user is always smarter than the founder.

(Still free to try — https://www.lancerwise.com)
```
