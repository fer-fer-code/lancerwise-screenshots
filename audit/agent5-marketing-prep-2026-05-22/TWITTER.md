
**Reply 3 — "What's your stack?"**
```
Next.js 14 + Supabase (Postgres w/ Row-Level Security) + Vercel. AI is multi-provider (Gemini Flash, Claude Haiku, Groq) routed by task. Payments via LemonSqueezy. PWA on the frontend so it works as an installable app.
```

**Reply 4 — "Is my data safe?"**
```
RLS at the database layer means even if our app code has a bug, the DB itself blocks cross-tenant reads. Full GDPR export from settings — your data leaves with you if you ever want to leave. We never sell or share.
```
