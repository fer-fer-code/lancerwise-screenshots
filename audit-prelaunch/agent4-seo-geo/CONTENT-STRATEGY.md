# Content strategy plan — EN-market SEO blog

**Date:** 2026-05-29
**Scope:** 15 article themes for Lancerwise's `/blog` (EN-only per Ramiz decision)
**Status:** RESEARCH ONLY — plan for Ramiz to pick from. NOT writing posts yet.

---

## Why these themes

The current blog has **4 posts** in 4 of 7 declared categories (`Pricing`, `Invoicing`, `Contracts`, `Time Tracking`). The existing posts are GEO-strong (formulas in `<code>`, tables of actual numbers, section IDs) per the audit. The strategy below extends that quality bar across:

- **2 empty categories** — `Productivity`, `Taxes` (top of `categories` enum, no posts yet)
- **3 high-intent commercial topics** missing entirely — alternatives/comparisons, niche specialization, templates
- **Continuation in existing categories** (Pricing/Invoicing/Contracts each have only 1 post)

Search-landscape findings (from WebSearch probes):
- "HoneyBook alternative" / "Dubsado alternative" — well-established competitor terms; Bonsai et al. dominate. **Open territory if Lancerwise can claim "free / AI-native alternative".**
- "Hourly rate calculator" — already owned (we have one). Continue feeding it traffic.
- "Quarterly estimated taxes" / "self-employment tax" — massive evergreen search volume.
- "Freelance proposal template" / "winning proposal" — bottom-funnel intent.
- "Scope creep" / "kill fee" / "retainer agreement" — high-intent contract-pain queries.

## The 15 themes — ranked by leverage (recommendation in §recommend)

Each theme = (a) target query family, (b) draft H1, (c) why this works for Lancerwise specifically, (d) primary CTA target, (e) effort estimate.

### Tier A — high-leverage commercial intent (recommend ship first)

#### A1. **HoneyBook alternative — free, AI-native (Lancerwise honest comparison)**
- **Target queries:** "honeybook alternative", "honeybook vs", "cheaper than honeybook", "free honeybook"
- **H1 draft:** "HoneyBook Alternative: Why Freelancers Switch to LancerWise (Free Tier, AI Contracts, No 1.5% Processing Fee)"
- **Why us:** HoneyBook charges $39/mo + 1.5% Stripe processing on top. Lancerwise has $0 free tier (2 clients) + $15 Pro. Direct cost comparison is concrete + honest.
- **CTA:** /upgrade (Pro tier)
- **Effort:** **Medium** (need accurate competitor pricing snapshot, 6-8 feature-by-feature rows, screenshots)

#### A2. **Dubsado alternative — simpler, AI-built-in**
- **Target queries:** "dubsado alternative", "dubsado vs", "easier than dubsado"

- **Why us:** Dubsado's workflow automations have a steep learning curve per market signal. Lancerwise's onboarding is 10-min per existing content.
- **CTA:** /upgrade
- **Effort:** **Medium**

#### A3. **Bonsai alternative — accounting/tax + invoicing simpler**

## Why NOT in plan (and why)

- **"Best freelance tools 2026" listicles** — every CRM-blog already ranks for these. Lancerwise can't outrank Bonsai/HoneyBook blogs on their own backyard. Skip.
- **"How I built X" personal stories** — high-effort low-yield until Ramiz has shipping-success narrative worth telling.
- **AI tool reviews** — too generic, gets buried.
- **Industry trends / 2026 predictions** — fad content, no compound asset.
