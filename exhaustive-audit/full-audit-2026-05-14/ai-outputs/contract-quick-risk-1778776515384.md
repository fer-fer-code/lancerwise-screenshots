WEB DEVELOPMENT CONTRACT

1. Parties and Project Overview

This Web Development Contract ("Agreement") is entered into on May 14, 2026, by and between Phase Ten Test ("Freelancer"), with its principal place of business, and Maria Rodriguez ("Client"), with its principal place of business. The Freelancer and the Client are collectively referred to as the "Parties." The Parties agree to collaborate on a web development project, with the Freelancer providing services to the Client as outlined in this Agreement.

2. Scope of Work

The Freelancer agrees to provide the Client with a brand identity package, including but not limited to: (i) logo design with three concepts, (ii) brand guidelines document outlining typography, colors, and voice, (iii) social media templates set, and (iv) business card design. The scope of work includes two revision rounds and the delivery of final files in vector and raster formats.

3. Deliverables and Timeline

The Freelancer shall deliver the following milestones and deliverables within the specified timeline:
- Logo design concepts: Week 2
- Brand guidelines document: Week 4
- Social media templates set: Week 6
- Business card design: Week 7
- Final files in vector and raster formats: Week 8
The total duration of the project shall be eight weeks from the effective date of this Agreement, May 14, 2026. The Freelancer shall use reasonable efforts to meet the aforementioned deadlines.

4. Payment Terms

The Client agrees to pay the Freelancer a total amount of $6,500 USD for the services outlined in this Agreement. The payment schedule is as follows:
- 30% deposit ($1,950 USD) due upon signing of this Agreement
- 40% payment ($2,600 USD) due at the midpoint of the project (Week 4)
- 30% payment ($1,950 USD) due upon completion of the project (Week 8)
All payments shall be made in USD, and the Client shall be responsible for any applicable taxes or fees.

5. Revisions Policy

The Freelancer includes two revision rounds for the deliverables outlined in this Agreement. The Client shall provide feedback and revisions within a reasonable timeframe, allowing the Freelancer to complete the project within the specified timeline. Any additional revisions beyond the included two rounds shall be subject to an additional fee, to be agreed upon by the Parties.

6. Intellectual Property

The Freelancer retains ownership of all intellectual property rights, including but not limited to copyrights, trademarks, and trade secrets, in and to the deliverables until full payment has been made by the Client. Upon receipt of full payment, the Freelancer assigns to the Client all rights, title, and interest in and to the deliverables, except for the Freelancer's moral rights.

7. Confidentiality

The Parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all information, whether written or oral, that is disclosed during the term of this Agreement. The Freelancer and the Client shall not disclose any confidential information to any third party without the prior written consent of the other Party.

8. Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice to the other Party in the event of a material breach that remains uncured for a period of seven days. In the event of termination, the Freelancer shall be entitled to payment for all work completed prior to termination, and the Client shall pay the Freelancer for all outstanding invoices.

9. Limitation of Liability

In no event shall either Party be liable for any indirect, special, or consequential damages, including but not limited to loss of profits, arising out of or in connection with this Agreement. The Freelancer's total liability for damages shall be limited to the total amount paid by the Client under this Agreement.
